Biography

Mr. Yongheng Wang is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. He received his Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2021 and earned a Master's degree from Tsinghua University in 2024. His research primarily focuses on the control and optimization of power systems.
